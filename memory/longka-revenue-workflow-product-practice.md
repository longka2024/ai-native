# Longka Revenue Workflow Product Practice

Hard rule: for customer-facing products, do not start from AI content factory. Start from revenue workflow.

Correct customer sequence:

1. AI Sales Champion: extract winning sales language from converted and unconverted conversations.
2. AI Private Domain CRM: build customer memory and daily follow-up workflow from chat history.
3. AI Content Factory: generate content only from real sales scripts, objections, FAQ, customer profiles, and conversion cases.

Internal Longka sequence remains:

1. collect cases and expert workflows
2. identify pain and paying users
3. manually validate
4. agentize workflow
5. package as industry workflow product

Customer-facing names should use business language, e.g. AI Sales Champion, AI Private Domain Assistant, AI Content Partner. Avoid selling "toolbox", "crawler", "agent framework", or "knowledge base".

Every customer-facing feature must answer: how does this help reply, follow up, convert, or save time?
