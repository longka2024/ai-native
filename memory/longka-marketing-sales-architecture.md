# Longka Marketing And Sales Architecture

Longka is not a toolbox. It is an AI Native industry workflow product factory.

Current clarified architecture:

- Market radar: for Longfei to discover opportunities, collect cases, and decompose expert workflows.
- AI Content Factory: the "ying" side of marketing, for Xiaomei/operators/business owners to produce daily Moments, Xiaohongshu, image cards, articles, short videos, Remotion/Hyperframes videos, and later digital human content.
- AI Sales Champion Private-Domain Assistant: the "xiao" side, for conversion after traffic exists; extracts sales scripts, customer profiles, objections, follow-up actions from chat history.
- Industry workflow product factory: packages proven workflows into sellable products.

Important correction:

`private-domain-toolkit-xiaomei-final-runtime` is an active AI Content Factory deliverable and must continue. It should not be replaced by the AI Sales Champion direction.

Content factory brings people in. Sales assistant converts people into money. They share data later but should not be mixed into one MVP too early.

Current priority:

1. Finish Xiaomei content factory runtime package.
2. Integrate previous promo video automation stack, including Remotion/Hyperframes and later digital human workflow.
3. Add feedback tracking for content performance.
4. Build AI Sales Champion assistant later from real private-domain conversations.

Two product principles to keep applying:

1. Frontend must stay simple. Backend may be complex. The user sees results, the system handles orchestration.
2. A real AI Agent must deliver near-immediate ROI through business outcomes, not chatbot-style interaction.

Customer-facing language should be business language, not technical language. Avoid selling "crawler", "toolbox", "agent framework", "knowledge base", or "CLI".
