## 2026-05-19: Video production exposed the need for operator-grade workflow

Saved source:

- `E:\Codex\LONGKA_VIDEO_WORKFLOW_ASSISTANT_PRD.md`
- `E:\Codex\my-video\docs\plans\2026-05-19-video-production-workbench-design.md`
- `E:\Codex\my-video\docs\plans\2026-05-19-video-production-workbench.md`

Evolution:

- Manual generation of five promo videos revealed repeatable failure points: weak templates, asset mismatch, wrong copy, layout QA, music mismatch, missing covers, and too many command-line steps.
- The product requirement became a workflow workbench: Xiaomei should review scripts, select templates, generate covers, choose music, render, and QA from a page.
- The lesson generalizes beyond video: every AI production workflow needs approval gates and visible intermediate artifacts.

## Current Synthesis

The project has evolved from:

```text
make a useful color-report product
```

to:

```text
build a reusable AI Native base system that discovers evidence, lets humans decide, runs agent workflows, produces business deliverables, and compounds learning across verticals.
```

The current high-confidence path:

1. Keep personal image/color report as the real commercial vertical.
2. Turn video production into a standardized content-factory surface.
3. Build revenue workflow from real private-domain and sales material.
4. Use Market Radar to choose the next verticals from evidence, not intuition.
5. Persist memory and decisions in files so future sessions do not restart from the current chat window.
