## Decisions

### 2026-05-15: AI Native means judgment first, execution second

Source:

- `E:\Codex\AI_NATIVE_STARTUP_PLAYBOOK.md`
- `C:\Users\longfei\.codex\memories\ai-native-startup-playbook.md`

Decision:

- The scarce resource is no longer "can we build it?" but "should we build it?"
- New products should follow evidence -> scope -> architecture -> MVP -> launch -> feedback -> scale.
- Do not jump from idea directly to code just because Codex can build fast.

### 2026-05-16: Longka is an AI Native industry workflow product factory

Source:

- `E:\Codex\LONGKA_AI_NATIVE_COMPANY_PLAYBOOK.md`

Decision:

- Longka is not a generic toolbox.
- The system packages repeated, high-value industry work into runnable, reusable, deliverable, sellable AI workflows.
- The base layers are data, analysis, workflow, production, delivery forms, and publishing/review.

### 2026-05-18: Marketing and sales are two connected but separate tracks

Source:

- `E:\Codex\LONGKA_MARKETING_AND_SALES_PRODUCT_ARCHITECTURE.md`
- `C:\Users\longfei\.codex\memories\longka-marketing-sales-architecture.md`

Decision:

- Content Factory brings people in.
- Sales Champion / Private-Domain Assistant converts people into money.
- They should share data over time, but should not be merged into one MVP too early.

### 2026-05-18: For paying business owners, revenue workflow can precede content factory

Source:

- `E:\Codex\LONGKA_REVENUE_WORKFLOW_PRODUCT_PRACTICE.md`
- `C:\Users\longfei\.codex\memories\longka-revenue-workflow-product-practice.md`

Decision:

- External customer-facing products should often start from sales champion extraction and private-domain CRM.
- Content becomes stronger after extracting real sales language, customer objections, customer profiles, FAQ, and conversion cases.

### 2026-05-19: Video production must become an operator workbench, not command-line craftsmanship

Source:

- `E:\Codex\LONGKA_VIDEO_WORKFLOW_ASSISTANT_PRD.md`
- `E:\Codex\my-video\docs\plans\2026-05-19-video-production-workbench-design.md`

Decision:

- Xiaomei should not operate Figma, Remotion commands, or scattered scripts directly.
- The workflow should be: generate script -> human review -> choose template -> match assets -> generate cover -> choose music -> render -> QA -> export.
- Templates, covers, music, and QA are first-class workflow parts, not afterthoughts.
